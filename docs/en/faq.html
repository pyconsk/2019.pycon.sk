Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

Where will the conference happen?

Slovak University of Technology in Bratislava
Faculty of Informatics and Information Technologies
Ilkovičova 2
842 16 Bratislava 4

What languages are spoken?

Talks will be mostly in English and Slovak language. Possibly we will have a few talks in Czech language. If you do not speak Slovak or Czech do not worry, we have two tracks so there will be always talk in English.

Volunteers do speak English. In Slovakia there is mandatory second language in school so most of the attendees will speak English or other language (at least a little bit).

Tickets

What is the difference between self funded (individual rate) and full-price (corporate rate) ticket?

Both tickets are valid for full three conference days. Including everything mentioned on tickets page. Difference is if you pay for the ticket yourself or your employer (company). For those who pay for them selves we are trying to be more inclusive so anybody can afford the ticket. This way we are able to support teachers and students for whom the price might be too high.

Companies can add the tickets to their costs.

I don't have PayPal account, is there any other way to buy a ticket?

Currently, it is not possible to pay via PayPal without a Paypal account.

Therefore we accept also a wire transfer. This method, however, requires manual processing, which may lead to little delay. Please send us a list of tickets you are interested in, we will send you variable symbol and after we receive the payment, we will issue and send the requested tickets to you by email.

We also accept Bitcoin, Litecoin and (possibly) other crypto currencies. Please send us a list of tickets you are interested in, and currency you want to use and we'll send you a wallet address and once the payment is confirmed, we will issue and send the requested tickets to you by email.

What is included in the ticket?

We made a list of things what you can expect at the conference on the tickets page.

Do you offer group discounts?

If you would like to buy more than 15 tickets please contact us by email at info@pycon.sk.

Is there any another possibility to get to PyCon?

Yes submit a talk, and if your talk is selected you will receive a speakers ticket for free.

If you are less than 15 years old and come accompanied by a parent, you can just purchase one ticket only for the parent, but you have to inform us in advance to issue an another ticket for the child.

Will there be last moment tickets?

Last 50 tickets will be more expensive, so don't leave your ticket purchase to last moment!

Do you have social event in the evening?

On Firday there will be an unofficial party. We will meet in a pub and have fun, no need for special ticket.

On Saturday we'll have an official conference dinner! There is a special ticket for this event.

Is my conference ticket valid for the FRIDAY unofficial party?

YES. You don't have to purchase a ticket for the FRIDAY unofficial party. In the pub everybody pays for themself.

Is my conference ticket valid for the SATURDAY conference dinner (official social event)?

NO. You have to purchase a ticket for the SATURDAY conference dinner (official social event). Ticket to the dinner includes dinner in a form of a buffet and a welcome drink. You pay for all other drinks.
