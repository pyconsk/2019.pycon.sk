Call for volunteers

We need you to help organize PyCon SK 2019!

PyCon SK is organized by the Python community and for the Python community. All of our staff members are volunteers. We are looking for a new members to join the team of organizers. This is great opportunity to meet people, make new friends and get the opportunity to learn new things.

As a volunteer you get a free ticket to the conference as a thank you for making the change in our society. If you spend more than 40 hours during the year as a volunteer you can give 3% to any registered non-profit in your tax return.
